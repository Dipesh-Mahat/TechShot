Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a story idea or tip?

You can email our editorial team at editors@techshot.com with your idea or tip. Please include as much relevant information as possible, including sources and why you think this story would be interesting to our readers.

Do you accept guest articles or contributions?

Yes, we consider guest articles from technology experts and professionals. Please review our contribution guidelines before submitting your pitch.

How can I advertise on TechShot?

For advertising opportunities, please contact our advertising team at ads@techshot.com. We offer various options including banner ads, sponsored content, and newsletter promotions.

I found an error in an article. How can I report it?

We strive for accuracy in all our reporting. If you've found an error, please let us know by emailing corrections@techshot.com with details of the error and the article in which it appears.

Can I republish or reference TechShot content?

Our content is protected by copyright, but we do allow limited use with proper attribution. For permission to republish our content, please contact permissions@techshot.com.
